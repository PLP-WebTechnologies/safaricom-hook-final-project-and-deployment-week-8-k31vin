Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in front-end web development, creating responsive websites and web applications. My services include HTML/CSS development, JavaScript programming, responsive design, website optimization, and basic UI/UX design.

How much does a website cost?

Website costs vary depending on the scope, complexity, and features required. I offer custom quotes based on your specific needs. Contact me with your project details for a free estimate.

What is your typical project timeline?

Simple websites can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects may take 4-8 weeks. Timeline depends on project scope, requirements, and revision rounds. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site updated, secure, and running smoothly. These can include regular updates, performance optimization, content changes, and technical support.
